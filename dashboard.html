YouTube Gaming Trend Dashboard

A PostgreSQL-backed dashboard that tracks which games appear in trending YouTube gaming video titles.

How to read this dashboard:
Each update scans the top trending YouTube gaming videos in the US. If a known game title or alias appears in a video title, it counts as one appearance. For example, if Roblox appears in 4 out of 25 trending video titles, it is shown as “Appears in 4 of 25 trending videos.”

The change column compares the latest update against the previous update, similar to a stock page. Higher appearances mean the game is showing up more often in trending gaming content.
